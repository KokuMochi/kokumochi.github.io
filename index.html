Our Manifest

❤️ More Than Just Prints – Building a Dream with My Daughter

It all started with a simple request from my 10-year-old daughter. She drew her own creative ideas on paper and asked, "Dad, can you turn these into a clicker?" When I saw the joy on her face as I brought her sketches to life, I realized this isn't just about plastic – it's about pure fun. That’s how our family team was born: our daughter is the mastermind behind the ideas, I handle the mechanics to ensure every clicker works perfectly, and my wife adds the soul by hand-painting every detail.

🆓Why do we do this, and why for free?

Because we believe in the power of sharing joy. Our clickers aren’t just "projects for a drawer." We want you to use them and play with them!

🎯 Our Goal: An AMS for our daughter

We won't hide it – we have a dream. Every dollar of your support goes directly toward buying an Automatic Material System (AMS). With it, our daughter will be able to print her visions in full color, and my wife's brushes will finally get a well-deserved rest. We’re building this together.

💡 To Sellers and Businesses – A Manifest of Integrity

Listen, in a world full of restrictions and lawyers, we choose a different path. There is no "3D printing police" here. You have complete freedom to print, test, and sell these designs to grow your own business. I put my faith directly in your conscience and human honor. If our projects bring success and profit to your shop, I ask you simply, human to human: share what you can. Support our Patreon. Help a hobbyist dad build something lasting for his daughter. You aren’t paying for files. You are buying a front-row seat on our journey. You are with us as new ideas are born, you see our passion turn into reality, and you are in the heart of this adventure. It’s your way to become part of what we create. Let's grow together, with mutual respect and a passion that cannot be measured.
